## SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

### For Webcam Setup:
1. Mount webcam on flexible stand
2. Position overhead (30-40cm above desk)
3. Angle camera downward at document
4. Connect USB to computer
5. Use Windows Camera app or browser
6. Adjust lighting and focus
7. Capture images

### For Smartphone Setup:
1. Install scanning app
2. Mount phone on overhead stand
3. Position 30-40cm above desk
4. Open scanning app
5. Place document below
6. App auto-detects and captures
7. Save as PDF or image

---

## TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

**Lighting:**
- Use natural light from window (best)
- Add desk lamp if needed
- Avoid shadows on document
- Position lights at 45° angles

**Camera Position:**
- 30-40cm above document
- Directly overhead (not angled)
- Stable mount (no shaking)
- Test before buying

**Document Placement:**
- Flat surface
- Contrasting background (dark desk for white paper)
- Align document straight
- Remove clutter around document

**Software:**
- Use auto-crop features
- Enable auto-enhance
- Save as PDF for multiple pages
- Organize files by date/name
