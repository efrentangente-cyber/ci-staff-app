## 7. COST-SAVING TIPS

1. **Buy in Bulk:** Negotiate discounts for multiple tablets
2. **Refurbished Equipment:** Consider certified refurbished tablets (30-50% savings)
3. **Lease Options:** Monthly payment plans for expensive equipment
4. **Government Grants:** Check for cooperative development grants
5. **Shared Resources:** Share printers between departments
6. **EcoTank Printers:** Lower long-term ink costs
7. **Extended Warranties:** Protect investment with service plans
